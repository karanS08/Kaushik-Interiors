About

At Kaushik Interiors, we believe that every space has a story waiting to be told — your story. Our passion lies in transforming ordinary rooms into deeply personal sanctuaries that reflect the people who live and work within them. We specialize in warm, human-centered interiors that blend aesthetics with authenticity, comfort with creativity. Whether you're redesigning a single room or your entire home or office, our collaborative approach ensures that your vision, needs, and lifestyle are always at the heart of the design. From concept to finishing touches, we focus on the details that make a space uniquely yours. Led by a team of experienced designers and storytellers, we’re here to help you create more than just a beautiful interior — we’re here to craft a space that feels like home.
